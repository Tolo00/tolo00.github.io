About Me

I’m a student at the University of Skövde currently studying game development and programming.

On the side of my studies I have spent a lot of time developing different hobby projects, alone and with a team. This has given me a lot of experience working with different game engines and developing complex systems from scratch.

Currently I am specializing in creating tools for increased efficiency within my projects. These usually include tools for visualizing and manipulating object data.

Besides writing code, I enjoy playing games like Stardew Valley and Payday 2. I also like playing the guitar and staying active by going to the gym.
